# pronunciation: DLC -> "D L C"
Welcome to Two-Phase Direct Liquid Cooling Efficiencies and Fluids.

This course looks at why two-phase D L C is becoming part of the liquid-cooling conversation, what changes inside the cooling loop, and how dielectric fluids affect safety, compatibility, operations, and lifecycle planning.

We will stay practical. The goal is not to declare one cooling method the answer for every site. The goal is to help you recognize where two-phase belongs, what it asks of the design, and what evidence operators should expect before scaling it.
