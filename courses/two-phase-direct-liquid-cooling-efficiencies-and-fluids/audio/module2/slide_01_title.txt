Module two: Two-Phase D L C Architecture and Efficiency Mechanics.

In module one, we framed why two-phase cooling is becoming relevant. Now we open the loop.

You will connect boiling and condensation to practical design choices: the cold plate, the vapor return, the C D U, pressure control, line sizing, and interoperability. By the end, you should be able to explain what changes inside the T C S and what can remain familiar at the F W S boundary.
