Module four: Deployment, Operations, Monitoring, and Lifecycle.

The last module is about making two-phase D L C operationally credible. A good design still has to be started correctly, filled correctly, monitored correctly, serviced safely, and documented well enough to survive refresh cycles.

We will focus on the operating signals and records that keep the T C S, C D U, and fluid lifecycle under control.
