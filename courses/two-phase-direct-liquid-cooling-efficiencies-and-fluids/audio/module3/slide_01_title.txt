# pronunciation: DLC -> "D L C"; ODP -> "O D P"; GWP -> "G W P"
Module three: Dielectric Fluids, Selection, and Compatibility.

Two-phase D L C depends on the working fluid. The fluid determines boiling behavior, safety classification, environmental profile, material compatibility, handling rules, monitoring needs, and lifecycle options.

This module uses the OCP dielectric heat transfer fluid guideline as the primary reference. We will look at the property envelope, the compatibility evidence, and the operational discipline needed to use these fluids responsibly.
