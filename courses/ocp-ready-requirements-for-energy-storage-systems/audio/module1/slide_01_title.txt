Welcome to O C P Ready Requirements for Energy Storage Systems.

This course is about E S S in data centers: where it is deployed, why it is changing, what hazards it introduces, and how the O C P white paper helps designers and operators plan safer deployments.

We will use the January twenty twenty-six white paper, the twenty twenty-six O C P EMEA Summit presentation, and the O C P Energy Storage Systems project as our anchors.

By the end, you should be able to read the E S S requirements with a practical eye: what applies to your architecture, what needs early review, and where local approval or insurance requirements can change the plan.
