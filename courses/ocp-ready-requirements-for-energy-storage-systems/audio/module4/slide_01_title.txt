Module 4: Codes, Standards, and O C P Ready Application.

This final module turns the white paper into a practical navigation tool.

We will look at why standards fragment across regions, how codes become enforceable locally, and why the authority having jurisdiction and insurer can change the deployment path.

Then we will connect the main standards families: N F P A, I C C, U L, C S A, I E C, FM Global, and regional frameworks such as the European Union Battery Regulation.

Finally, we will use the O C P Ready E S S matrix and a deployment workflow to show how project teams can move from use case to approval-ready design.
