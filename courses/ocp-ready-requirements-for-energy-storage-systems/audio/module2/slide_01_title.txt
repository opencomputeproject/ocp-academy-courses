Module 2: Deployment Models, Architectures, and Technologies.

In this module, we move from the reason E S S matters to the ways it can actually appear in a data center.

We will compare row, rack, sidecar, facility-level, microgrid, and edge deployments. Then we will classify architectures by electrical coupling, distribution, control, energy or power orientation, hybridization, and grid interaction.

We will also look at the technology landscape: electrochemical batteries, supercapacitors, capacitors, and storage types that are less common in data center operations.

By the end, you should be able to describe an E S S deployment clearly enough that the safety and compliance questions can follow.
