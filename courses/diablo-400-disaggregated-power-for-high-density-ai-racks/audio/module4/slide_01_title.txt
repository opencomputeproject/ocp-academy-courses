Optional Module 4. Future Work: Facility LVDC Power Distribution.

The required Diablo 400 course is complete. This optional module looks beyond the sidecar and asks what may come next.

The main source is the OCP DCF Power Distribution LVDC white paper, version one dot zero, dated March 30, twenty twenty-six. We will use it to connect Diablo 400 to broader low voltage DC distribution work: facility architecture, voltage bands, polarity, protection, conversion, energy storage, grid interaction, and standards gaps.
