Welcome to OCP Diablo 400: Disaggregated Power for High-Density AI Racks.

In this course, we will look at why power delivery is becoming one of the defining constraints for AI infrastructure, and how the Diablo 400 specification responds to that pressure. The focus is not on selling a particular implementation. It is on understanding the shared architecture, the vocabulary, and the technical requirements that let the OCP community reason about high-density rack power together.

We will begin with the problem Diablo 400 is solving: the move from conventional in-rack power toward a dedicated sidecar power rack.
