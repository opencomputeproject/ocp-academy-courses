Module 2. Diablo 400 Architecture and Power Path.

In Module 1, we looked at why high-density AI racks need a different power architecture. Now we move into the specification itself.

This module follows the power path through the Diablo 400 rack: the physical envelope, the AC input, ride-through and energy storage, the plus-minus 400 volts DC output, dynamic loading, and efficiency. By the end, you should know where the key requirements live and how the major electrical choices fit together.
