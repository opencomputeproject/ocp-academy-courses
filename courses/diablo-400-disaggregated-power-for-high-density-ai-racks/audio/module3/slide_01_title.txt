Module 3. Interfaces, Safety, Telemetry, and Adoption.

The previous module followed the Diablo 400 power path from AC input to plus-minus 400 volts DC output. This module turns that power path into a deployable rack architecture.

We will look at the interfaces that move power into and out of the sidecar, the grounding choices that shape fault behavior, the telemetry and environmental assumptions, and the safety, EMC, and sustainability requirements that keep the design grounded in real data center practice.
