Welcome to O C P "ee-sun": Ethernet for Scale-Up Networks. This is a short, dense course built around the "ee-sun" Base Specification, Revision one point zero, published by the OCP Networking Project and effective February ninth, twenty twenty-six. It's authored by Manoj Wadekar at Meta and Rajesh Sankaran at Microsoft, and it was shaped by a working group of one hundred sixty-six members spanning more than a dozen companies. We're going to move quickly. In about twenty minutes you'll know what "ee-sun" is, why the industry needs it now, exactly what changes at the wire, and what your hardware and your switches have to do to conform. Let's get into it.
