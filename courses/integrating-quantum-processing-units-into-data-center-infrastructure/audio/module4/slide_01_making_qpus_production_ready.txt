# pronunciation: QPU -> "Q P U"; QPUs -> "Q P U systems"; OCP -> "O C P"
Module four. From pilot deployment to O C P practice.

This final module is about turning early Q P U deployments into repeatable production patterns. The whitepaper's conclusion is direct: success depends on more than device performance.

Environmental control, power delivery, cooling, networking, storage, service processes, and standards all shape whether quantum systems can operate reliably at scale.

You'll connect those needs to existing O C P project areas, shared infrastructure ideas, operational expectations, and the workstream where the community can continue refining the model.
