# pronunciation: QPU -> "Q P U"; QPUs -> "Q P U systems"; HPC -> "H P C"
Module two. The logical Q P U and its classical stack.

In this module, you'll look inside the architecture that turns a physical quantum processor into something a data center can connect, schedule, observe, and operate.

The whitepaper uses a logical Q P U reference stack to explain the pieces. It includes a real-time host, a real-time interconnect, a quantum system controller, and the physical Q P U. Around those components sit H P C, A I, storage, networking, and management systems.

By the end, you'll be able to explain why quantum deployments are inherently hybrid.
