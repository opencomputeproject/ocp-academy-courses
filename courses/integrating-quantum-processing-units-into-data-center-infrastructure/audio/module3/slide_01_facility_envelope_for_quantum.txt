# pronunciation: QPU -> "Q P U"; QPUs -> "Q P U systems"
Module three. Facility readiness by modality.

The previous module described the logical and classical stack. This module asks a more physical question: what must the facility provide so a Q P U system can arrive, operate, and be serviced without turning the deployment into a one-off lab project?

You'll compare site selection, service boundaries, modality matrices, laser and vacuum systems, photonic systems, and cryogenic systems.

The key idea is that quantum requirements are not only inside the product. They also touch the room, the floor, the utilities, nearby equipment, access procedures, and operational responsibilities.
