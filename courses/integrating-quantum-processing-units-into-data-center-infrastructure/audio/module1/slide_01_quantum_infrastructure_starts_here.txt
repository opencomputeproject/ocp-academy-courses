# pronunciation: OCP -> "O C P"; QPU -> "Q P U"; QPUs -> "Q P U systems"; SCORM -> "scorem"
Module one. Why quantum belongs in the data center.

This course is about integrating quantum processing units, or Q P U systems, into data center infrastructure. The starting point is the O C P whitepaper, Integrating Quantum Processing Units into Data Center Infrastructure, dated April fourteenth, twenty twenty-six.

The goal is not to turn you into a quantum physicist. The goal is to help data center, cloud, H P C, and infrastructure teams understand what changes when quantum hardware becomes a production asset.

Press play first, then advance with arrow or Space when you're ready.
