# pronunciation: OCP -> "O C P"; QPU -> "Q P U"; QPUs -> "Q P U systems"
This course follows the path a deployment team would naturally take.

First, you'll see why O C P is treating quantum as infrastructure work, and why the industry needs common language before deployments scale. Then you'll look inside the hybrid stack that surrounds a Q P U system: classical compute, storage, networking, control, and environmental systems.

After that, the course turns physical. You'll compare facility readiness by modality. That means site selection, delivery, floor loading, power, cooling, vibration, humidity, service boundaries, and maintenance.

The final module brings those pieces back to O C P practice. You'll connect the whitepaper's recommendations to existing O C P projects, operational maturity, and the workstream where this work continues. The course uses the whitepaper as the primary source, with summit talks adding market context and field perspective.
