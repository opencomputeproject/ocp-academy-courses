# pronunciation: OCP -> "O C P"; QPU -> "Q P U"; QPUs -> "Q P U systems"
Module one set the frame for the whole course.

The central idea is simple: quantum deployment is becoming an infrastructure problem. The whitepaper does not ask data centers to learn every detail of quantum physics. It asks the community to define the conditions under which Q P U systems can be deployed, managed, maintained, and scaled.

You also saw why O C P is a natural home for this work. O C P has experience turning fragmented hardware practices into shared specifications, service boundaries, and operational patterns. Quantum now needs that kind of translation.

Finally, you saw why modality matters. Different physics creates different power, cooling, control, space, and environmental needs. That variability is the reason a common framework matters.
