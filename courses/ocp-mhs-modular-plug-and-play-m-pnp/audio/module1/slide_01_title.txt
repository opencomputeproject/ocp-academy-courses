Module one. Introduction to Modular Plug-and-Play, or M-PNP. In this module, we'll set up the problem M-PNP solves, walk through the vision for self-describing modular hardware, place the M-PNP workstream inside the broader DC-MHS family of specifications, and tour the contents of the version zero point five specifications bundle. By the end you'll know what M-PNP is, why it exists, and what's in the bundle. Let's get started.
