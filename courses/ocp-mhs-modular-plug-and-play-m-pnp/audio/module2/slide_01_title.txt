# pronunciation: Fru -> "Fru" (read as the word, not letters); BMC -> "B M C"; HPM -> "H P M"

Module two. The Fru, Discovery, and Boot specification. This is the operational core of the M-PNP bundle. It defines what Fru information a component must expose, the order in which the BMC discovers everything in the system, and the boot process requirements that let a BMC and a Bios bring a previously-unknown HPM up without prior knowledge. We'll spend the most time of any module here — there's a lot of mechanism to understand. Let's get into it.
