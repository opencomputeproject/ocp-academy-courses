This course is divided into three modules.

Module one sets the stage. We'll cover why high-performance CDUs are essential right now, and exactly where the Deschutes CDU sits in the broader liquid-cooling architecture.

Module two opens the unit and walks through it in detail — physical dimensions, the redundant pump and heat-exchanger arrangement, the full sensor suite, the controls strategy, and the real hydraulic and thermal performance curves published in the spec.

Module three covers deployment. We'll examine the Redmond Hot Aisle Containment interface that ships with the same specification, the field-replaceable units and maintenance approach, and the three-tier supplier model Google designed for global availability at competitive cost.
