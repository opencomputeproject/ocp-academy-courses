Welcome to Module three of the OCP Academy Project Deschutes course.

In the previous module we walked the inside of the CDU. Now we step outward — to how the unit gets deployed in a real data center, how it's maintained over its lifetime, and how Google's supply chain strategy makes it broadly available.

This module covers four topics: the Redmond Hot Aisle Containment interface that ships in the same specification document, the CDU's interaction with rack power distribution, the field-replaceable units and serviceability features, and the three-tier supplier model designed to drive worldwide availability at competitive cost.

We'll close the course with a clear set of resources and a call to action: how to engage with Project Deschutes from wherever you sit in the ecosystem.
