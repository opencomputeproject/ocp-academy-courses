Module two is about power, thermal design, and serviceability shifts.

The first module showed why AI infrastructure increases pressure on the optical layer. Now we will look at two responses.

The first response is moving optical conversion closer to the switching silicon, through near-packaged or co-packaged designs. The second response is changing connector behavior and service workflows, through expanded beam and rear blind-mate interfaces.

The common theme is that optics are becoming part of the system architecture, not just a module installed at the edge of the switch.
