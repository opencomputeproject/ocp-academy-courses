Welcome to Introduction to Emerging Optical Technologies.

This course is about the pressure AI infrastructure is putting on the physical optical layer. We will look at the problems that show up as clusters grow: more fiber, more ports, more heat, tighter latency budgets, and less tolerance for slow turn-up or slow repair.

The goal is not to predict one winning technology. The goal is to build a practical map. By the end, you should be able to hear terms like co-packaged optics, expanded beam, multicore fiber, hollow-core fiber, and QPU integration, and know which infrastructure constraint each one is trying to relieve.
