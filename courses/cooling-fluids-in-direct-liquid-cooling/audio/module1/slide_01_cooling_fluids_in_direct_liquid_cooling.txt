# pronunciation: DLC -> D L C; TCS -> T C S; CDU -> C D U
Welcome to Cooling Fluids in Direct Liquid Cooling. This course is about the fluid decisions inside the technology cooling system: the T C S loop between the C D U and the cold plates.

We will stay focused on single-phase direct-to-chip cooling: treated water and P G twenty-five. By the end, you should be able to ask better questions about compatibility, chemistry, filtration, monitoring, and records before the fluid ever reaches an expensive rack.
