No single document carries the whole operating answer. OCP guidance gives an open community framework. ASHRAE T C nine point nine provides data center and technology-space context. ASTM methods give repeatable ways to test corrosion, compatibility, and related properties. The supplier treatment plan fills in product-specific limits and corrective actions.

The practical point is that a learner should never rely on memory or tribal practice alone. A good fluid program ties each decision back to a named source: the OCP guideline, an ASTM method, a certificate of analysis, a safety data sheet, or a supplier-approved treatment plan.
