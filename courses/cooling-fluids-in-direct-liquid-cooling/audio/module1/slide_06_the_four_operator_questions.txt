Operator concerns are not concentrated in one place. Operators worry about picking a fluid that is compatible with their system. They worry about leaks around expensive hardware. They worry about monitoring the fluid and knowing what a sample means. And they worry about having enough design and operating expertise.

Those questions become the spine of this course. The right answer is rarely a single product statement. It is a chain of evidence: fluid chemistry, wetted materials, installation cleanliness, filtration, monitoring, trending, supplier support, and documented decisions.
