The first boundary is simple but important. This course is about the secondary closed loop that carries heat from the rack equipment to the C D U. In ASHRAE language, that loop is the technology cooling system, or T C S.

The facility water system is on the other side of the C D U heat exchanger. It matters to the total design, but it is not the same fluid environment. The T C S touches cold plates, manifolds, quick disconnects, hoses, pump seals, filters, reservoirs, and sensors. Every one of those wetted surfaces becomes part of the fluid compatibility question.
