Module four is about keeping the loop healthy after commissioning. A good fluid program does not wait for failure. It watches trends, compares them with the baseline, and makes controlled adjustments before a chemistry signal becomes downtime.

We will separate on-site tests from laboratory quality assurance, look at automated monitoring, interpret trends, discuss supplier-guided dosing, and handle the messy reality of fluid mixing. We will close with useful life, disposal, and the records that keep the next refresh from starting blind.
