Documentation is the connective tissue of fluid operations. The draft guidance calls for a file for each system with the identity and source of the heat transfer fluid, copies of fluid analyses, and a history of cooling-system components that were replaced.

It also calls for records of system volume, adjustments, and materials of construction. That volume record is not just accounting. It is critical for inhibitor addition. The water treatment plan should include operational parameters, testing schedule, and safety data sheets for all fluid products.

Good records make future troubleshooting faster and safer. Poor records force every operator to rediscover the loop.
