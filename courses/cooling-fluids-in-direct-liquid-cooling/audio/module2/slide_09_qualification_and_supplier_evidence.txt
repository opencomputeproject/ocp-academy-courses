A fluid selection should leave evidence behind. At minimum, the owner should keep the fluid identity, certificate of analysis, safety data sheet, treatment plan, and supplier recommendations. The fluid supplier, IT supplier, contractor, and service provider should align on compatibility with the wetted materials list and operating conditions.

This is especially important in a multi-vendor rack environment. A homogeneous supercomputer is one thing. A reusable liquid cooling infrastructure that may hold different hardware over multiple refresh cycles is another. Without evidence and records, each refresh recreates uncertainty.
