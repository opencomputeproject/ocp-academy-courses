By the end of this module, you will be able to describe why serviceability belongs in the mechanical design, not only in the maintenance manual.

You will be able to explain why the leak-check fluid itself must be compatible with the system, and why cold plates are isolated during part of the initial flush.

You will also be able to identify what not to introduce into the loop, what baseline records matter at startup, why sidestream filtration is recommended below five microns, and how biological growth can threaten cold plate microchannels.
