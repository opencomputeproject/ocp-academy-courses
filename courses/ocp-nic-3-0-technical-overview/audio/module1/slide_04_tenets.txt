The specification is built on five Open Compute tenets. Openness means the spec is IP-free and accessible to the entire industry. Efficiency drives a size-optimized footprint with thermally efficient and power-optimized operation. Impact is about creating a multi-vendor removable Nick ecosystem with a robust, competitive supply chain. Scalability ensures common remote management interfaces and forward-compatible architecture for next-generation designs. And sustainability is reflected in community-authored standards, cost-focused design, and the use of common materials. These five principles shape every design decision in the spec.
