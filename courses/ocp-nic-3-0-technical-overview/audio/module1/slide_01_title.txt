# pronunciation: NIC -> Nick; OCP NIC 3.0 -> OCP Nick three point zero

Welcome to the OCP Nick three point zero Technical Overview. This OCP Academy Nick course provides a comprehensive walkthrough of the OCP Nick three point zero design specification, version one point six point zero, released in March twenty twenty-five.

The specification was developed by the OCP Nick Subproject under the OCP Server Project. Across six modules, you'll cover form factors, mechanical design, electrical interface behavior, management, signal integrity, thermal and environmental requirements, compliance, and ecosystem resources. By the end, you'll have the technical foundation to read the specification with confidence and participate in implementation conversations.
