# review_status: upgraded for Leo v2 narration

The OCP Nick 3.0 form factor is not limited to network interface cards. It serves as a platform for diverse I/O devices. PCI retimer cards can provide signal conditioning for extended reach. Accelerator cards and co-processors can leverage the standardized hot swap slot. NVMe storage controllers and storage HBAs fit the same envelope. Precision timing cards for PTP and network synchronization are another option. And really, any P C I E device that fits the electrical and mechanical constraints can use this form factor. The key takeaway is that all these non-Nick use cases share the same slot infrastructure, enabling a unified approach across the data center.
