# review_status: upgraded for Leo v2 narration

Let's recap module one. OCP Nick 3.0 is an open IP-free specification enabling true multi-vendor interoperability for hyperscale data centers. It covers mechanical, electrical, thermal, and management requirements in one document. Four active form factors, SFF, TSFF, DSFF, and TDSFF, scale from 16 to 32 PCI Gen6 lanes and from 80 to 160 watts. The platform extends beyond Nicks to support accelerators, storage, retimers, and timing cards in the same slot infrastructure. 16 major companies drive the specification, and OCP Nick 3.0 is not backward compatible with MES 2.0. It introduces a new connector form factor and Gen6 interface.
