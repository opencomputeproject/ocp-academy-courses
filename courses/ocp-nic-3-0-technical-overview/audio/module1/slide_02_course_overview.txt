# review_status: upgraded for Leo v2 narration

This course is organized into six modules. Module 1, where we are now, introduces the specification and its goals. Module 2 covers form factors and mechanical design, the physical dimensions, connectors, and card assembly. Module 3 dives into the electrical interface and P C I E connectivity. Module 4 addresses management and firmware, including N C S I and Fru E E Prom. Module 5 covers signal integrity, thermal design, and environmental requirements. And module 6 wraps up with compliance, the OCP ecosystem, and what is coming next. The full course runs about 30 to 40 minutes and is aimed at a broad OCP community audience.
