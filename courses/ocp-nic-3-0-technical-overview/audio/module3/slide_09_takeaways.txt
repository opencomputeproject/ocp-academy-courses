# pronunciation: 4C+ -> four C plus; PCIe -> P C I E; DSFF -> D S F F; PRSNTB -> presence B; FRU EEPROM -> Fru E E Prom

Let's recap Module three. The four C plus connector carries P C I E data, reference clocks, resets, power, management, Wake, and OCP Bay sideband signals. S F F uses one Primary connector. D S F F extends lane capacity through Primary and Secondary connector positions.

The new integration model to keep is signal ownership. Some signals are driven by the baseboard, some are exposed by the card, and some are shared sideband paths that depend on implementation.

Bifurcation combines pins and discovery. For D S F F, presence B encoding and Fru E E Prom OEM records tell the baseboard which link subdivisions are available. Finally, four power states are mandatory for normal operation, Programming Mode is optional, and hot swap remains mainly a platform-side implementation.
