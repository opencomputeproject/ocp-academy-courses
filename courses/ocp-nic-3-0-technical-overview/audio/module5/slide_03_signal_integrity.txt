# review_status: upgraded for Leo v2 narration

P C I E signal integrity is a shared responsibility between the baseboard and the Nick card. The specification defines a channel budget that is split into two portions. Each side must meet its allocation for reliable P C I E Gen 6 operation at 64 gigatransfers per second. The 100 MHz differential reference clock is always supplied by the baseboard through the connector. AC coupling capacitors are placed on the Nick's receive pins to block DC bias voltage. To validate each side independently, the specification defines two compliance fixtures. The CLB, or compliance load board, tests the baseboard side. The CBB, or compliance breakout board, tests the Nick card side. If both sides meet their budgets, the combined channel will meet the P C I E Gen 6 specification.
