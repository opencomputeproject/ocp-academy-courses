# review_status: upgraded for Leo v2 narration

The specification provides a reference SFF heat sink sized at 65 by 45 millimeters designed around a typical 20 by 20 millimeter Nick ay-sick die. This is the largest heat sink that fits within the SFF card outline. At 150 linear feet per minute airflow, the reference heat sink supports approximately 12 watts of ay-sick power in a hot aisle configuration at 55 degrees Celsius inlet. In a cold aisle configuration at 35 degrees Celsius inlet, the same heat sink supports approximately 19 watts. That 20-degree drop in inlet temperature translates to roughly 60% more ay-sick power headroom. Total card power includes voltage regulators, memory, and optical transceivers, not just the ay-sick.
