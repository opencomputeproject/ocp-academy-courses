The specification defines 12 cooling tiers to standardize thermal expectations. Tiers one through six cover typical server deployments with standard fan configurations. Tier seven and above are for higher power cards requiring enhanced heat sinks or increased airflow. The cooling tier is declared in the Fru E E Prom, allowing the baseboard to adjust fan speeds at insertion time. The cooling tier table maps inlet temperature against each tier, specifying the minimum required airflow in linear feet per minute. For thermal validation, a standardized test fixture replicates server airflow conditions. Three candlestick temperature sensors are positioned at the card inlet. The fixture attaches to a wind tunnel for calibrated airflow. CFD models and fixture designs are published on the OCP wiki.
