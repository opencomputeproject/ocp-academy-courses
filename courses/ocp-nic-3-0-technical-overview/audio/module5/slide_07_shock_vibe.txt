# review_status: upgraded for Leo v2 narration

Shock and vibration testing is mandatory for all OCP Nick 3.0 cards. The shock test uses a vertical shock table with controlled impulses applied to the installed card assembly. The SFF fixture tests four cards simultaneously for efficient batch validation. The DSFF fixture also holds four cards, scaled to the larger form factor dimensions. Beyond shock and vibration, the specification requires gold finger durability verification using a dye and pull test. This destructive test applies colored dye to the gold plating and pulls a calibrated probe across the contact surface, revealing plating defects, adhesion failures, or wear patterns. This validates reliable electrical connection over repeated insertion and removal cycles.
