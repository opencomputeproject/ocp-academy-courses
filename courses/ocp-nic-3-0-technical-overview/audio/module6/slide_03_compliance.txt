OCP Nick 3.0 cards must meet several required certifications. Rose restricts the use of hazardous substances in electronic components. REACH is the European Union's comprehensive chemical safety regulation. FCC Part 15 Class A sets electromagnetic emissions limits for commercial devices in the United States, and EN 55032 Class A is the European equivalent. IEC 62368-1 covers equipment safety requirements for IT hardware. IEC 61000-4-2 specifies electrostatic discharge immunity. Beyond these required certifications, three are recommended: WEE for end-of-life recycling, CISPR 32 for international EMI compliance, and VCCI for Japan's voluntary EMI standard. These certifications apply to the Nick as a component. System-level certification is the server manufacturer's responsibility.
