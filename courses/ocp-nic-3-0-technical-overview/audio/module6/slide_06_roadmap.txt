# review_status: upgraded for Leo v2 narration

The specification continues to evolve. In the near term, thermal characterization for TSFF, DSFF, and TDSFF form factors will be completed. 1.6 Terabit Ethernet is targeted for next-generation network bandwidth. P C I E Gen 7 will bring the next signaling rate increase, and liquid cooling solutions for the TSFF form factor are under development. Looking longer term, enhanced DPU form factors with expanded power envelopes are being explored. Liquid cooling for the TDSFF form factor is also on the roadmap. These future directions reflect the data center industry's trajectory toward higher bandwidth, higher power, and more advanced cooling strategies.
