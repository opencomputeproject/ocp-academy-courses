Welcome to module two of the OCP Nick three point zero Technical Overview, Form Factors and Mechanical Design. In this module, we move from the high-level overview into the physical design of the card. We will cover dimensions, connectors, faceplates, and card assembly, the mechanical details that make OCP Nick 3.0 a field-replaceable multi-vendor standard. This module is based on the mechanical sections of the OCP Nick 3.0 Design Specification version version one point six point zero.
