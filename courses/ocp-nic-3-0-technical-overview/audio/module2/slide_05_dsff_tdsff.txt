# pronunciation: DSFF -> D S F F; TDSFF -> T D S F F; SFF -> S F F; 4C+ -> four C plus; PCIe -> P C I E

D S F F and T D S F F are the dual-width path. A D S F F card uses Primary and Secondary four C plus connector positions, supports up to 32 P C I E lanes, and can receive up to 160 watts at the card edge across the two connector positions.

The mechanical idea is specific. A D S F F slot is made from two adjacent S F F positions after the middle guide rail is removed. The connector pitch is 81.55 millimeters, which is smaller than two normal adjacent S F F slots. That is why some S F F faceplate assumptions do not automatically carry over.

Also keep the insulator point straight. The card-side insulator or backing plate is part of the card construction. It is not a spacer that gets added between an already-built card and the baseboard during installation.
