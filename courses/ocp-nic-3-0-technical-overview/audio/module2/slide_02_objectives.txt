# pronunciation: SFF-TA-1002 -> S F F T A one thousand two; 4C+ -> four C plus

By the end of this module, you'll be able to identify the key dimensions of each OCP Nick three point zero form factor and explain the differences between S F F, T S F F, D S F F, and T D S F F.

You'll understand the S F F T A one thousand two, four C plus gold-finger connector and its two-stage mating sequence. You'll also be able to describe faceplate configurations and the three card retention mechanisms defined by the spec. Finally, you'll know how D S F F cards relate to adjacent S F F slots on the baseboard, without confusing card-side insulators with baseboard installation steps.
