# pronunciation: RBT -> R B T; MCTP -> M C T P; NC-SI -> N C S I; FRU EEPROM -> Fru E E Prom

Let's recap Module four. Three management types are defined: R B T plus M C T P, R B T, and M C T P. Every compliant card must support at least one, and R B T plus M C T P is preferred because it combines N C S I behavior with richer platform management.

N C S I gives the B M C control and pass-through traffic over the sideband path. The arbitration ring prevents bus contention, and R B T isolate prevents a powered-down slot from dragging the bus low. The Fru E E Prom is mandatory and carries the OEM records that declare card identity, lane subdivision, power, cooling tier, and capability information. Temperature reporting and U E F I F M P support complete the operational management picture.
