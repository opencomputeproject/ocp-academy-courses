# review_status: upgraded for Leo v2 narration

Three monitoring and update mechanisms keep OCP Nick 3.0 cards healthy. For temperature, any card with five watts or more TDP must report the temperature with plus or minus three degrees Celsius accuracy. Three thresholds are defined. Warning indicates the Nick is approaching its thermal limit. Critical means corrective action is needed, and fatal signals imminent damage risk with optional self-shutdown. For power, requirements are declared in the Fru E E Prom. The baseboard verifies the slot can supply required power before transitioning to main power. For firmware, every card must support UEFI FMP for standardized updates. NIST 800-193 is recommended for authenticated firmware images, rollback protection, and secure boot chain verification.
