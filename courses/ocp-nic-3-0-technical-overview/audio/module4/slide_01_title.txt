Welcome to module four of the OCP Nick three point zero Technical Overview Management and Firmware. In this module, we focus on how the baseboard management controller communicates with the Nick and how the Nick identifies itself to the system. Management interfaces are what make OCP Nick 3.0 cards truly plug and play. Without them, a baseboard would have no way to discover, configure, or monitor an installed Nick.
